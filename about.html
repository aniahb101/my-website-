About

Hello! I'm Aniah Brown and I am 24. I am a passionate individual who embarked on a journey into the world of development in 2023. My fascination with computers and the art of editing ignited the spark that led me to explore the dynamic and ever-evolving field of web development.

As someone who has always been captivated by the intricate workings of computers, I found myself drawn to the possibilities that coding and programming offered. The idea of creating, shaping, and optimizing digital experiences appealed to my curiosity and desire to contribute to the vast digital landscape.

In addition to my love for computers, my hobby of video and photo editing played a pivotal role in my journey. The creative process of crafting and refining visuals honed my attention to detail and cultivated a deep appreciation for the aesthetics of design. This, combined with the technical aspects of development, provided me with a holistic perspective on bringing ideas to life in the digital realm.
